Hello! My name is Shelley Eang (pronounced “Aye-ng”) and I am a graduating senior studying in Human Centered Design & Engineering at the University of Washington. My passion for user experience design grew after seeing my non tech-savvy family members grow closer and more connected through real time video calls. Through my professional pursuit, I can help bring a better experience to populations in overlooked communities and to those who want to gain connectivity through user-centered design.


As a user experience practitioner, I strive to empathize with the users and design with the user in mind through using user-centered design principles and best practices in every step of the design process. My specialties include interaction design, visual design, and improving UX. While I enjoy designing aesthetics and user-friendly designs, I also love facing new challenges and learning new things!


In my free time, I enjoy traveling, cooking, and crafting DIY projects. I have been vegan for a few years and I often visit communities and travel outside of Washington to see and become more aware of the local, organic, and vegan scene. In the past year, I visited Vietnam, Southern California, Oregon, and Canada. There are many places I still have not explored but plan to in the future! I also enjoy taking on new challenges in cooking healthy delicious meals for those who aren’t vegan and practicing sustainability through living minimal and lowering food waste. I also enjoy DIY projects that reuse, recycle, and focus on natural products.
